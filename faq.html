FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and pricing.

General Questions

What services does AMNEXA Technologies offer?

+

AMNEXA Technologies offers a comprehensive range of digital services including web development, app development, Django development, React development, WordPress development, Shopify development, SEO services, graphic design, video editing, AI solutions, and data analytics. We provide end-to-end solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Where is AMNEXA Technologies located?

+

AMNEXA Technologies is based in Indore, India. However, we serve clients globally with our remote-first approach. Distance is never a barrier to quality service delivery.

How long has AMNEXA Technologies been in business?

+

AMNEXA Technologies has been serving clients for over 5 years, delivering high-quality digital solutions to businesses across various industries worldwide.

Services & Pricing

How much does a website project cost?

+

Project costs vary based on requirements, complexity, features, and timeline. We provide custom quotes after understanding your specific needs through a free consultation. Our pricing is competitive and transparent with no hidden costs.

Do you offer fixed-price or hourly billing?

+

We offer both fixed-price and hourly billing options depending on the project scope. For well-defined projects, we provide fixed-price quotes. For ongoing or evolving projects, hourly billing may be more suitable.

What payment terms do you offer?

+

Typically, we work with a milestone-based payment structure: 30% upfront, 40% at design approval, and 30% on completion. For long-term projects, we can discuss alternative payment schedules.

Development Process

What is your development process?

+

Our development process includes: 1) Requirement analysis and planning, 2) UI/UX design, 3) Development, 4) Testing and quality assurance, 5) Deployment, and 6) Ongoing support. We maintain transparent communication throughout with regular updates.

How long does it take to complete a project?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website may take 2-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 2-6 months. We provide estimated timelines during the initial consultation and keep you updated on progress.

Will I own the source code?

+

Yes, you will own the source code and all intellectual property for your project upon final payment. We provide complete source code, documentation, and deployment files.

Support & Maintenance

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

+

Yes, we provide ongoing support and maintenance services to ensure your website or application runs smoothly. We offer various support packages based on your needs, including bug fixes, updates, security patches, and feature enhancements.

What happens if I need changes after project completion?

+

We offer a warranty period for bug fixes after project completion. For additional changes or new features, we can provide quotes on an hourly or project basis. We're always available to support your evolving needs.

Technology & Security

What technologies do you use?

+

We use modern, industry-standard technologies including Django, React, Python, WordPress, Shopify, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, and more. We choose the right technology stack based on your specific requirements and project goals.

How do you ensure project security?

+

We follow security best practices including secure coding standards, regular security audits, SSL implementation, data encryption, secure authentication, and regular updates. We also provide security recommendations for ongoing maintenance.
